Lakeburg

Lakeburg is a first-person psychological horror experience that thrusts you into an atmosphere of total vulnerability. After waking up disoriented in a strange house, your only priority is to survive an environment that seems to twist around you. With no weapons or light to guide your way, you must navigate claustrophobic corridors and uncover the secrets hidden in the darkness before the entity stalking you finds you.
